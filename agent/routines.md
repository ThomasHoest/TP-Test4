# Your assistant's routines

These are things I can do on a recurring schedule. The scheduler
isn't live yet — when you toggle one on, I'll remember your
preference and start running it when scheduled delivery ships.

## Daily morning digest
**enabled: false** · default time: 07:30

A short summary of what to focus on today — pulled from your tasks and recent notes. Surfaces blockers and suggests one next action.

## Weekly priorities review
**enabled: false** · default time: Monday 09:00

A look back at last week's progress and forward at this week's priorities. Opens a 5-minute conversation if you reply.

## Monthly reflection
**enabled: false** · default time: 1st of month, 17:00

Reads through the month's notes and drafts, surfaces patterns or questions worth thinking about.

## Quarterly goals review
**enabled: false** · default time: 1st of quarter, 09:00

Compares quarterly goals against actual outcomes; suggests adjustments for next quarter.
